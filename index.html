About Me

Currently Im focusing on putting togeather all of the things I can do in one place so people can see it. I am a visual artist, optimisation specialist, and a creative coder based in Chicago, IL. I have a passion for creating unique and engaging digital experiences that blend art and technology.

With a background in visual arts and a keen interest in programming, I strive to push the boundaries of creativity through my work. My projects often explore the intersection of art and technology, resulting in innovative and thought-provoking pieces.

My journey as an artist has been shaped by my experiences and the diverse influences that surround me. I believe in the power of art to inspire, provoke thought, and connect people across different cultures and backgrounds.

As an optimisation specialist, I am dedicated to improving processes and enhancing user experiences. I enjoy finding creative solutions to complex problems and leveraging technology to achieve optimal results.

Whether it's through visual art, coding, or optimisation, I am committed to creating meaningful and impactful work that resonates with audiences. I invite you to explore my portfolio and join me on this creative journey.

Thank you for visiting my website. I hope you find inspiration and enjoyment in my work. If you have any questions or would like to collaborate, feel free to reach out.

In my free time, I enjoy music art and all thing tech. I also love to read and get inspiration for my work from different cultures and experiences.
